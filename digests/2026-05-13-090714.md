**Andrej Karpathy** (former Director of AI at Tesla, OpenAI co-founder, PhD from Stanford)

Karpathy made a compelling case that the default output format for AI is about to shift away from markdown and toward HTML. His argument rests on a simple but under-appreciated asymmetry: audio is the preferred input channel for communicating *with* AI, but vision is the preferred output channel for receiving information *from* AI -- roughly a third of our brains are dedicated to visual processing, making it a "10-lane superhighway of information." He sketches the progression: raw text is hard to read, markdown is a modest improvement, HTML offers far more flexibility in layout, graphics, and interactivity. His practical tip right now: end your query with "structure your response as HTML" and open the generated file in a browser. He also notes that richer input modalities are still needed -- pointing and gesturing at things on screen, the way you would with a colleague sitting beside you -- before the full human-AI communication loop is realized.

Karpathy 提出了一个有说服力的观点：AI 输出的默认格式即将从 Markdown 切换到 HTML。他的论点建立在一个简单却常被忽视的不对称性上：与 AI 交流时，语音是首选的输入方式；而从 AI 接收信息时，视觉才是首选的输出通道——我们大约三分之一的大脑专门用于视觉处理，使其成为"信息进入大脑的十车道高速公路"。他勾勒出一个演进路径：纯文本难以阅读，Markdown 是小幅改进，HTML 则在布局、图形和交互性上提供了更大的灵活性。他目前的实用建议是：在你的问题末尾加上"structure your response as HTML"，然后用浏览器打开生成的文件。他还指出，在人与 AI 之间的完整沟通循环得以实现之前，还需要更丰富的输入方式——比如能够指向屏幕上的内容、做出手势，就像坐在你身旁的同事一样。

**No Priors: Amex Global Business Travel: The World's First AI Take Private with Long Lake CEO Alexander Taubman**

**The Takeaway:** AI-native private equity is proving that owning businesses -- rather than selling software to them -- is the fastest way to turn AI into real economic value.

Long Lake Management CEO Alexander Taubman makes a contrarian argument that most AI companies are thinking about go-to-market wrong. The standard Silicon Valley playbook is to identify an industry ripe for disruption, build software, and sell it as a vendor. Taubman and his team tried something different: buy the businesses outright, then transform them from the inside using an AI platform they built called Nexus. After ~30 acquisitions across industries like homeowners association management, specialty tax, and HR services, the results are hard to dismiss. Companies that were growing at 0-5% annually before Long Lake acquired them are now growing at 20%+ -- not by cutting costs, but by making employees dramatically more productive and driving better customer outcomes.

The Amex Global Business Travel acquisition -- a proposed $6.3B deal for a 111-year-old company that predates commercial aviation -- is being called the world's first AI take private. Taubman's reasoning is that travel is a "mission-critical, high cost of failure" service where AI can have an outsized impact. Approximately 80% of the Nexus platform is shared across all of Long Lake's verticals, which is what makes rapid deployment possible: what once took over a year to stand up now happens within days of closing an acquisition.

The most striking insight is about employee retention. Taubman describes Long Lake's AI tools as having the same pull as email: "If you now leave Long Lake to go to a competitor, you have to start doing all this mundane work again -- 25% to 30% of your day. And the thought of it is like giving up email." That stickiness lets Long Lake pay its people more, attract better talent, and grow faster -- a flywheel the company is now betting can apply to one of the world's largest corporate travel platforms.

**核心洞察：** AI 原生私募股权正在证明，直接收购企业而非向其出售软件，是将 AI 转化为真实经济价值的最快路径。

Long Lake Management CEO Alexander Taubman 提出了一个反主流的观点：大多数 AI 公司在市场拓展策略上方向错了。硅谷的标准打法是找到一个亟待颠覆的行业，建立软件，然后作为供应商销售出去。Taubman 和他的团队则选择了一条不同的路：直接收购企业，再从内部借助自研 AI 平台 Nexus 进行改造。历经约 30 次跨行业收购——涵盖业主协会管理、专项税务和人力资源服务等——成果令人信服。被 Long Lake 收购前年增长率在 0-5% 的企业，如今年增长率已达到 20% 以上。驱动这一增长的，不是裁员降本，而是大幅提升员工生产力、改善客户体验。

对 Amex Global Business Travel 的收购计划——一笔涉及这家有着 111 年历史、甚至早于商业航空业的企业的 63 亿美元交易——被称为全球首例"AI 私有化"。Taubman 认为，企业出行是"关键任务、容错成本极高"的服务，AI 在其中可以发挥超乎寻常的价值。Nexus 平台约 80% 的能力在 Long Lake 旗下各业务线之间共享，这正是快速部署成为可能的原因：原本需要一年多才能完成的落地工作，现在在完成收购后几天之内即可实现。

最令人印象深刻的洞察来自员工留存率。Taubman 将 Long Lake 的 AI 工具比作电子邮件——一旦用上就无法回头："如果你现在离开 Long Lake 去竞争对手那里，你就得重新开始做那些繁琐的工作，占用你每天 25% 到 30% 的时间。那种感觉就像要你戒掉电子邮件一样。"这种黏性让 Long Lake 能够支付更高薪酬、吸引更优秀的人才、实现更快的增长，形成一个良性飞轮——而这家公司正打赌，同样的逻辑可以复制到全球最大企业出行平台之一。
