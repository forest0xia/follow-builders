**The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck**
*Stripe's AI Chief: How AI Agents Will Buy, Sell, and Pay*

**The Takeaway:** AI is enabling a new class of solopreneurs and micro-firms — with agents not just automating purchases but poised to run entire businesses end-to-end.

Emily Shense, Head of Data and AI at Stripe, has a front-row seat to the real agentic economy through Stripe's data, which spans roughly 2% of global GDP. Her core argument: the shift from per-seat SaaS subscriptions to usage-based billing isn't just a monetization preference — it's existential for AI companies, because every inference call carries a real marginal cost. "How your customers use your product directly determines whether you make or lose money" — something that was never true in SaaS.

Her most underrated point is token theft. Fraudsters don't bother stealing payment credentials anymore; they just steal tokens, which they resell, use to generate fake music for royalty farming, or wrap into their own competing products. "More than one in six signups at AI companies are this kind of abuse." Unlike SaaS piracy — which carries near-zero cost to the victim — AI token theft directly erodes margins and can collapse unit economics. Three main patterns have emerged: multi-account abuse to farm free-tier credits, free trial drain with payment methods that are never charged, and end-of-month invoice ghosting at machine speed.

What stands out in the macro data: solopreneurs are exploding as a category. There are now 5 million people in the US earning their primary income running one-person companies, with hundreds of thousands clearing over $1M/year. Stripe's 2026 Atlas cohort is tracking to five times the revenue of last year's class at the same stage. The new playbook — build with AI, deploy with AI, reach customers globally from day one — is rewriting what it takes to start a company. New business registrations are up 40% in the Netherlands, 70% in Finland, and 80% in France.

Looking twelve months ahead, Shense expects the first real examples of agents as multi-faceted economic actors: not just buying, but provisioning infrastructure, selling services, and running businesses end-to-end. "It's not Emily permissioning an agent to buy on her behalf. It's Emily has an agent who's tasked with running a business — and that includes buying some things and selling some things and making some profits."

Stripe is building the infrastructure for this world: the Agentic Commerce Protocol (co-created with OpenAI), Link Wallet for agents with programmable spend controls, shared payment tokens that keep credentials away from agents entirely, Stripe Radar extended to full customer lifecycle fraud, and Stripe Projects for vibe deployment — orchestrating Vercel, Supabase, Cloudflare, and 16+ partners so agents can deploy apps without human setup wizards.

**要点：** AI 正在催生一批新的独立创业者和微型企业，agent 不仅仅在自动化购买行为，而是有望端到端地运营整个业务。

Emily Shense 是 Stripe 数据与 AI 负责人，她通过 Stripe 的数据——覆盖全球 GDP 约 2%——对真实的 agentic 经济有着前排视角。她的核心判断：从按座位订阅转向基于使用量计费，不只是商业模式的选择，对 AI 公司来说是生死攸关的问题。因为每一次 inference 调用都有真实的边际成本。"你的客户如何使用你的产品，直接决定了你是赚钱还是亏钱"——这在 SaaS 时代从来不是问题。

她最被低估的洞察是 token 盗窃。欺诈者已经不需要盗取支付凭证，直接盗取 token 就够了——可以转卖、用来批量生成假音乐刷版税，或者包装成自己的竞品产品出售。"AI 公司超过六分之一的注册行为属于这类滥用。"与 SaaS 盗版（受害者几乎零成本）不同，AI token 盗窃会直接侵蚀利润率，甚至让单位经济彻底崩溃。目前主要有三种模式：批量注册多账户以薅免费额度、用虚假支付方式薅免费试用期，以及在月末以机器速度耗尽额度后消失不付款。

宏观数据中令人瞩目的一点是：独立创业者这个群体正在爆发式增长。美国目前有 500 万人靠经营独立公司维持主要收入，其中数十万人年收入超过 100 万美元。Stripe 2026 年 Atlas 队列在同等阶段的营收，是去年班级的五倍。新的打法——用 AI 构建、用 AI 部署、从第一天起就覆盖全球市场——正在重写创业的门槛。新企业注册量在荷兰增长了 40%，芬兰增长了 70%，法国增长了 80%。

展望未来 12 个月，Shense 预期将出现首批真正的 agent 作为多功能经济参与者的案例：不只是购买，而是同时负责采购基础设施、销售服务、端到端运营业务。"不是 Emily 授权 agent 代替她购物，而是 Emily 有一个 agent 被委托去经营一家企业——包括购买一些东西、出售一些东西、实现盈利。"

Stripe 正在为这个世界构建基础设施：与 OpenAI 共同创建的 Agentic Commerce Protocol、带有可编程支出控制的 Link Wallet for agents、让 agent 完全接触不到真实凭证的 shared payment token、扩展至完整客户生命周期的 Stripe Radar 反欺诈体系，以及用于 vibe deployment 的 Stripe Projects——整合 Vercel、Supabase、Cloudflare 等 16 家以上合作伙伴，让 agent 无需人工操作即可完成应用部署。
