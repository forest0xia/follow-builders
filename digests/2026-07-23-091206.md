**AI & I by Every — "How Every's Team Used AI to Ship Its Biggest Launch Ever"**

**The Takeaway:** A small team used Claude, Codex, and a coordinated AI stack to pull off the largest subscription revenue increase in Every's history — and the person who orchestrated the email campaign did it by going to the gym.

Every launched "All Access," a $625/year tier bundling ten AI tool benefits — $1,000 in OpenAI Codex credits, 12 months of Cursor, access to Anthropic, PostHog, Render, Framer, Notion, and more — and generated record subscription revenue within a week. The real story is how the team executed.

When the 24-hour early-bird launch was underway, team member Austin didn't sit at his computer to build targeted email campaigns. He dropped a Slack screenshot of the plan into a Codex thread, typed "/LFG" to trigger Every's compound engineering plugin, and went to the gym. By the time he changed into workout clothes, Codex had already: created four audience-segmented cohorts in the email tool Kit, drafted personalized copy for each, run it through Spiral (Every's AI writing app) to match brand voice, pulled high-performing images from past sends, and scheduled the emails. Those four sends drove over $25,000 in revenue the next morning. "I was like, that's basically what it should do," Austin said. "My brain is so cooked on this stuff now."

On the product side, team member Yash has been rebuilding Sparkle — Every's product — 15 times using Claude and PostHog, and is automating the entire A/B testing pipeline so he can focus on the interesting decisions rather than clicking through dashboards. "I feel Claude Max is insane. I couldn't work without it." His philosophy: boring work gets automated; interesting work is what to test and when to act on results.

Creative director Douglas framed the shift as going from playing an instrument to conducting an orchestra: "You can conduct lots of pieces at once." He uses Claude for research and competitive auditing, and Flora for design, compressing weeks of brand strategy work into hours.

The team's advice for new builders: dupe a product you love using as many Builder Pack tools as possible; pick the project you'd be most excited to text a friend about; or rebuild your portfolio — it proves you're AI-native and gives you instant real-world feedback.

**AI & I by Every — 《Every 团队如何用 AI 打造史上最大发布》**

**核心要点：** 一个小团队利用 Claude、Codex 和协调的 AI 工具栈，完成了 Every 历史上最大规模的订阅收入增长——而负责邮件营销的那个人，是在去健身房的途中完成这一切的。

Every 推出了「All Access」，一个年费 625 美元的会员层级，捆绑了十项 AI 工具权益——1,000 美元的 OpenAI Codex 积分、12 个月 Cursor、Anthropic、PostHog、Render、Framer、Notion 等工具的访问权限——并在一周内创下了订阅收入记录。真正的故事在于团队是如何执行的。

在产品层面，团队成员 Yash 已经用 Claude 和 PostHog 把 Every 的产品 Sparkle 重建了 15 次，并正在将整个 A/B 测试流水线自动化，这样他就可以专注于有趣的决策，而不是点击仪表盘。「我觉得 Claude Max 太逆天了，没有它我根本没法工作。」他的哲学是：无聊的工作交给自动化，有趣的工作是决定测什么、什么时候采取行动。
