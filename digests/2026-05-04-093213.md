**Aaron Levie** — CEO of Box

Box CEO Aaron Levie wrote what may be the most detailed breakdown yet of why enterprise agent adoption is harder than it looks — and why that's a massive opportunity. He argues the complexity of implementing agents in large organizations will create more work than anyone currently imagines, spanning five distinct problem areas: connecting agents to legacy data securely, implementing proper access controls and entitlements, documenting processes in agent-readable form, redesigning human-agent workflows from scratch, and keeping up with rapidly evolving architectural best practices. "Just replicating the old workflow will mute the gains," he writes. This is his rationale for why vertical AI agents — those that go deep in a specific business domain — have a structural advantage. In a separate post, he added a philosophical note: "In general, we should treat AI like a utility, not like a being. The more we confuse what AI is the more we will make ourselves go crazy with analogies that will never fully hold true."

**Aaron Levie**——Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie 写下了迄今为止对企业 agent 落地难度最详尽的分析——以及这其中蕴含的巨大机会。他认为在大型组织中实施 agent 的复杂性将创造出超出所有人预期的工作量，涉及五个独立问题领域：安全地将 agent 连接到传统数据、实施适当的访问控制与权限管理、以 agent 可读的形式记录业务流程、从头重新设计人机协作工作流，以及跟上快速演进的架构最佳实践。他写道："仅仅复制旧的工作流程会削弱收益。"这也是他认为垂直 AI agent——那些深耕特定商业领域的 agent——具有结构性优势的理由。在另一条推文中，他补充了一个哲学观点："总体而言，我们应该把 AI 视为一种工具，而非一个生命体。我们越混淆 AI 的本质，就越会被那些永远无法完全成立的类比逼得抓狂。"

**Training Data: Andrej Karpathy: From Vibe Coding to Agentic Engineering**

**The Takeaway:** Andrej Karpathy draws a sharp line between vibe coding (raising the floor for everyone) and agentic engineering (preserving professional quality at greater speed) — and argues that the best agentic engineers today are not 10x but dramatically more productive than that.

Karpathy — former OpenAI co-founder, Tesla Autopilot architect, and the person who coined "vibe coding" — sits down with Training Data for a wide-ranging conversation about what it actually means to build software in 2026. He describes December 2025 as the inflection point when agentic tools stopped merely assisting and started *just working* — producing large chunks of code that he stopped correcting entirely.

His reframing of paradigms is clarifying: Software 1.0 is writing code; Software 2.0 is training neural networks on data; Software 3.0 is prompting — where the context window is your lever over an LLM interpreter. A striking example: OpenClaw's install script isn't a bash script, it's a text block you copy-paste to your agent. The app is the prompt.

He introduces "verifiability" as the key variable determining where AI will move fastest. Domains with strong verification signals — math, code, certain games — see outsized AI capability because labs can throw reinforcement learning at them. His most memorable illustration of AI's remaining jaggedness: state-of-the-art models can refactor 100,000-line codebases or find zero-day vulnerabilities, yet "will tell you to walk to a car wash 50 meters away."

On vibe coding vs. agentic engineering: *"Vibe coding is about raising the floor for everyone in terms of what they can do in software... agentic engineering is about preserving the quality bar of what existed before in professional software."* The key human skills that remain? Taste, aesthetics, judgment, and system design. The agents are the interns; humans are still the architects.

He also challenges hiring practices: puzzle interviews are dead. The right way to hire an agentic engineer is to give them a large project, watch them build and secure it, then send agents to try to break it.

**Training Data: Andrej Karpathy: 从 Vibe Coding 到 Agentic Engineering**

**核心洞见：** Andrej Karpathy 在 vibe coding（为所有人提升下限）和 agentic engineering（在更快速度下保持专业质量）之间划出了清晰的界线——并认为当今最优秀的 agentic 工程师的生产力提升远不止 10 倍。

Karpathy——OpenAI 联合创始人、Tesla Autopilot 架构师、"vibe coding"一词的创造者——在 Training Data 播客中进行了一次深度对谈，探讨 2026 年软件开发的真实面貌。他将 2025 年 12 月描述为 agentic 工具从辅助走向"直接有效"的拐点——大段代码输出后他已经完全停止手动修正。

他对编程范式的重新定义非常清晰：Software 1.0 是写代码；Software 2.0 是在数据上训练神经网络；Software 3.0 是 prompting——上下文窗口是你控制 LLM 解释器的杠杆。一个生动的例子：OpenClaw 的安装脚本不是 bash 脚本，而是一段你复制粘贴给 agent 的文本。应用即 prompt。

他提出"可验证性"是决定 AI 在哪些领域发展最快的关键变量。具有强验证信号的领域——数学、代码、某些游戏——会获得超出比例的 AI 能力，因为实验室可以对其施加强化学习。他对 AI 残余"参差性"最令人印象深刻的例证：最先进的模型能够重构十万行代码库或发现零日漏洞，却"会告诉你步行去 50 米外的洗车店"。

关于 vibe coding 与 agentic engineering 的区别：*"Vibe coding 是为所有人提升软件能力的下限……agentic engineering 是在更快速度下保持专业软件原有的质量标准。"* 人类保留的核心技能？品味、审美、判断力和系统设计。Agent 是实习生；人类仍然是架构师。

他还挑战了传统招聘方式：算法题面试已经过时。评估 agentic 工程师的正确方式是给他们一个大型项目，观察他们如何构建并保障其安全，然后派 agent 去尝试攻破它。
