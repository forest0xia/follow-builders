## OFFICIAL BLOGS

**Claude Blog: "Building intelligent apps for Apple platforms with Claude in the Foundation Models framework"** — Anthropic today released a new Swift package integrating Claude into Apple's Foundation Models framework. Apple developers can now use on-device models for simple, fast tasks like summarization and extraction, then hand off seamlessly to Claude for more complex workflows: multi-step reasoning, code generation, web search, and data analysis. Because Apple's framework returns typed Swift values via `@Generable` annotations, developers arrive at the Claude API call with clean, structured inputs. The package supports streaming Claude responses directly back into the same view, letting the two models function as a unified system.
https://claude.com/blog/claude-for-foundation-models

**Claude 官方博客：《在 Apple Foundation Models 框架中使用 Claude 构建智能应用》** — Anthropic 今日发布了一个新的 Swift 包，将 Claude 集成至 Apple 的 Foundation Models 框架。苹果开发者现在可以用设备端模型处理摘要、提取等轻量快速任务，再无缝将复杂工作交给 Claude：多步推理、代码生成、网页搜索及数据分析。得益于苹果框架通过 `@Generable` 注解返回类型化 Swift 值，开发者在调用 Claude API 时已拥有结构清晰的输入数据。该包还支持将 Claude 响应直接流式返回同一视图，让两个模型协作如同一体。

## PODCASTS

**The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck — "Cloudflare CEO: The Internet's Business Model Is Dead"**

**The Takeaway:** Bot traffic officially outnumbered human traffic on the internet in the first half of 2026 — arriving 18 months ahead of predictions — and it forces a complete rethinking of the internet's core economics.

Matt Turck interviews Matthew Prince, co-founder and CEO of Cloudflare, the company that sits in front of a vast share of global internet traffic. That vantage point gives Prince unusually clear visibility into macro trends, and what he's seeing is a structural inflection: AI agents are now driving more internet traffic than humans. The reason: where a human might visit five websites to shop for a camera, an agent visits 5,000. Extrapolating the current curve, Prince predicts a thousand times more bot than human traffic within five years.

The core problem is the funding model. The internet's ad-based business has been stable for 28 years — because humans click on ads. Bots don't. As agents increasingly act on behalf of users, the revenue model that funds the entire web breaks down. What replaces it is, Prince says, "totally undefined."

On Cloudflare's AI product journey: the company evolved from firewall-in-the-cloud into what Prince describes as a "next-generation AWS," largely by solving its own infrastructure problems. Cloudflare Workers, the developer platform that came out of the need for safe, lightweight code deployment, is now the foundation for edge AI inference. Cloudflare provides inference on open-source and frontier models alike — OpenAI's mobile app and Anthropic's cloud.ai both run on Cloudflare — with its 1,000+ data center edge network delivering lower latency and cost than going directly to AI providers.

Most provocatively, Prince defends Cloudflare's decision to cut more than 20% of its workforce — not because the business is struggling, but because AI eliminates the need for layers of middle management. He argues that waiting is the cruelest option: job markets for displaced workers will be far worse in 6–12 months when the rest of the industry follows. "I think that in the next six to twelve months, almost every company is going to go through some exercise like this."
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UN47z_opfmo

**MAD Podcast（主持人 Matt Turck）——"Cloudflare CEO：互联网的商业模式已经死了"**

**核心观点：** 2026 年上半年，bot 流量已正式超过互联网上的人类流量——比预测提前了约 18 个月——这迫使整个互联网重新审视其核心经济逻辑。

Matt Turck 对话 Cloudflare 联合创始人兼 CEO Matthew Prince。Cloudflare 坐拥全球相当大比例的互联网流量，这一独特位置让 Prince 对宏观趋势有着极为清晰的洞察——他看到的是一场结构性转变：AI agent 驱动的网络流量已超过人类。原因在于：人类可能只访问 5 个网站来选一台相机，而 agent 会访问 5000 个。按当前趋势外推，Prince 预测五年内 bot 流量将是人类流量的一千倍。

核心问题是商业模式。互联网基于广告的盈利逻辑稳定运行了 28 年——因为人类会点击广告。而 bot 不会。随着 agent 越来越多地代替用户行动，支撑整个互联网运转的收入模式正面临根本性的崩解。用 Prince 的话说，"什么来替代广告，完全没有答案"。

在 Cloudflare AI 产品方向上：公司从"云端防火墙"演变为他所称的"下一代 AWS"，很大程度上是在解决自身基础设施问题的过程中顺势而生。Cloudflare Workers 开发者平台——最初因安全部署代码的迫切需求而诞生——如今已成为边缘 AI 推理的基础。Cloudflare 为开源及主流前沿模型提供推理服务，OpenAI 的移动应用和 Anthropic 的 cloud.ai 均运行于 Cloudflare 之上，其遍布全球逾 1000 个数据中心的边缘网络可提供比直连 AI 服务商更低的延迟和成本。

最具争议的是，Prince 为 Cloudflare 裁减逾 20% 员工的决定进行了辩护——并非因为业务承压，而是因为 AI 消除了中间管理层存在的必要性。他认为，等待才是最残忍的选择：6-12 个月后，当其他公司纷纷跟进裁员，被裁员工所面临的就业市场将远比现在艰难。"我认为，在未来 6 到 12 个月内，几乎每家公司都将经历类似的人员调整。"
