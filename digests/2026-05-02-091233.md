**Anthropic Engineering: An update on recent Claude Code quality reports**

Anthropic Engineering disclosed three separate bugs that quietly degraded Claude Code's quality between March and April 2026, affecting Sonnet 4.6, Opus 4.6, and Opus 4.7. First, on March 4, the default reasoning effort was silently downgraded from high to medium to reduce latency — a tradeoff users rejected, prompting a revert on April 7. Second, a caching optimization shipped March 26 had a bug: instead of clearing old thinking history once after a session went idle, it cleared Claude's reasoning on every subsequent turn, making the model forgetful, repetitive, and prone to odd tool choices. This also drained usage limits faster than expected. Fixed April 10. Third, a system prompt instruction added April 16 — "keep text between tool calls to ≤25 words, final responses to ≤100 words" — caused a measurable 3% intelligence drop and was reverted April 20. As of April 23, Anthropic reset usage limits for all subscribers and is implementing tighter controls on any future system prompt change that trades intelligence for efficiency.
https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/april-23-postmortem

Anthropic Engineering 披露了三个独立 bug，导致 Claude Code 在 2026 年 3 月至 4 月间悄然降质，影响范围涵盖 Sonnet 4.6、Opus 4.6 和 Opus 4.7。第一个问题：3 月 4 日，默认推理强度被悄悄从 high 降至 medium 以减少延迟，但用户明确拒绝这一取舍，促使团队在 4 月 7 日恢复原设置。第二个问题：3 月 26 日上线的缓存优化存在 bug，原本应在会话空闲后一次性清除旧思维记录，却变成每轮对话都清除一次，导致模型变得健忘、重复，并做出奇怪的工具调用决策，同时还异常消耗 usage 配额，已于 4 月 10 日修复。第三个问题：4 月 16 日添加的一条系统 prompt 指令——"工具调用之间的文字不超过 25 词，最终回复不超过 100 词"——导致可量化的 3% 智能下降，已于 4 月 20 日撤销。截至 4 月 23 日，Anthropic 已向所有订阅用户重置 usage 配额，并对未来任何涉及智能与效率取舍的系统 prompt 变更实施更严格的管控。

**No Priors: Baseten CEO Tuhin Srivastava on the AI Inference Crunch, Custom Models, and Building the Inference Cloud**

**The Takeaway:** The AI inference market is still in the first 1% of its potential scale — enterprise adoption is the massive wave that hasn't hit yet.

Tuhin Srivastava, CEO of Baseten, runs one of the fastest-growing infrastructure companies in AI. Baseten grew 30x in a single year and is on track to exceed $1 billion in revenue. His company serves the fastest-growing AI application companies — Abridge, Cursor, Open Evidence, Decagon — and through them, he's watching the enterprise transition in real time.

The counterintuitive insight: 95% of the tokens Baseten serves run on custom, fine-tuned models — not vanilla open-source or frontier APIs straight from the lab. Enterprises and application companies aren't just calling a generic endpoint. They're building specialized models trained on proprietary workflows and user signals, and that's where lasting competitive moat gets built.

On the existential question of whether independent AI application companies can survive against frontier labs, Srivastava is clear: the application layer will exist because companies that gather unique user signal — deep inside clinical workflows, support queues, or financial processes — can post-train models on that reward signal and build moats that labs simply can't replicate. On Chinese open-source models: he doesn't see material security risk if models are network-isolated, and argues that blocking access would be "a massive loss" for US innovation. At peak, DeepSeek ran at roughly 20% the cost of comparable closed models with better latency. Raising the cost floor of intelligence slows down everything built on top of it.

"Everyone comes from capability first because that's really where the economic growth is being unlocked, where the value is being delivered, and then they optimize."
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XAbKflCncDo

**核心洞见：** AI inference 市场距离其潜在规模的上限仍不足 1%——企业大规模采用的浪潮尚未真正到来。

Baseten CEO Tuhin Srivastava 掌管着 AI 领域增速最快的基础设施公司之一。Baseten 在一年内实现了 30 倍增长，有望突破 10 亿美元年收入。他的公司服务于增速最快的 AI 应用公司——Abridge、Cursor、Open Evidence、Decagon——通过这些客户，他正在实时见证企业级 AI 的转型过程。

一个反直觉的洞见：Baseten 服务的 token 中，95% 运行在经过定制化微调的模型上——而非直接调用通用开源模型或前沿实验室的 API。企业和应用公司并不只是在调用一个通用接口，他们正在基于自身的私有工作流和用户信号构建专属模型，而这才是真正的竞争壁垒所在。

对于独立 AI 应用公司能否在前沿模型实验室的竞争下存活这一核心问题，Srivastava 的答案明确：应用层会一直存在，因为那些深度嵌入临床工作流、支持队列或金融流程、能收集独特用户信号的公司，可以基于这些奖励信号对模型进行后训练，构建任何实验室都无法复制的护城河。关于中国开源模型：他认为，只要模型在网络隔离环境中运行，安全顾虑并不构成实质性威胁，并表示限制使用这些模型将是"美国创新的巨大损失"。DeepSeek 在性能高峰期的推理成本仅为同等闭源模型的约 20%，且延迟更低。提高 AI 算力的成本下限，会拖慢所有建立其上的创新速度。

"所有人一开始追求的都是能力，因为这才是经济价值真正被释放的地方，才是价值真正被交付的地方，成本优化只是之后的事。"
