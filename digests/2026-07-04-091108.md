## PODCASTS

**The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck — "Why NVIDIA Is Giving Away AI Models | Bryan Catanzaro"**
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Oojrfdl42LI

**The Takeaway:** Open source AI isn't playing catch-up to closed source — it's playing a different and arguably more important game, and NVIDIA's Bryan Catanzaro explains exactly why.

Matt Turck sits down with Bryan Catanzaro, who leads Nemotron, NVIDIA's family of open foundation models. The timing is notable: Nemotron 3 Ultra just became the top-ranked US open weights model, and open source AI is accelerating with powerful new models arriving almost weekly.

Catanzaro's central argument is that open AI technologies matter for the same reason the open Internet mattered over closed networks like AOL or Prodigy. The transformation of retail, healthcare, and manufacturing by the Internet happened because openness allowed diverse applications. AI, he argues, will need that same diversity. Any single company's closed model cannot serve every domain optimally.

On the gap between open and closed source: Catanzaro pushes back on the framing of open source "catching up" to closed source. He argues this is the wrong question because capabilities are not monolithic — open models may trail in some dimensions while surpassing in others, and the gap is narrowing faster than most observers realize.

The episode goes deep into the technical architecture of Nemotron: four-bit training, hybrid Mamba-Transformer architecture, mixture of experts, multi-token prediction, and multi-teacher distillation — all explained in plain language. Catanzaro also offers a rare look inside how NVIDIA runs its AI research organization: getting hundreds of brilliant researchers to converge on one model rather than producing 100 separate papers.

His most thought-provoking line: we have an external stomach — we call it a kitchen. We are now creating an external brain. The implications are profound, and, as he admits honestly, nobody yet fully knows what they are.

**MAD Podcast 与 Matt Turck — "NVIDIA 为何要免费开放 AI 模型 | Bryan Catanzaro"**
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Oojrfdl42LI

**核心洞察：** 开源 AI 并非在追赶闭源——它在玩一场不同的、也可以说更重要的游戏。NVIDIA 的 Bryan Catanzaro 解释了其中的原因。

Matt Turck 与 Bryan Catanzaro 展开深度对话，后者负责 NVIDIA 旗下 Nemotron 开源基础模型系列。时机意味深长：Nemotron 3 Ultra 刚刚跻身美国开放权重模型排名第一，而开源 AI 正在加速，强大的新模型几乎每周都在涌现。

Catanzaro 的核心论点是：开放的 AI 技术的重要性，与当年开放的互联网胜过 AOL 或 Prodigy 等封闭网络的理由相同。互联网之所以能变革零售、医疗和制造业，正是因为开放性允许了多样化的应用。他认为，AI 同样需要这种多样性。任何一家公司的闭源模型都无法在所有领域都做到最优。

关于开源与闭源之间的差距：Catanzaro 对"开源在追赶闭源"的框架提出质疑。他认为这个问题本身就问错了，因为能力并非铁板一块——开源模型在某些维度可能落后，在其他维度或许领先，而且差距正在以多数观察者未曾意识到的速度缩小。

这一集还深入介绍了 Nemotron 的技术架构：四比特训练、混合 Mamba-Transformer 架构、Mixture of Experts、多 token 预测以及多教师蒸馏——全程以通俗语言讲解。Catanzaro 还罕见地展示了 NVIDIA AI 研究组织的运作方式：如何让数百名顶级研究者在一个模型上合力协作，而不是各自发表 100 篇独立论文。

他最发人深省的一句话：我们有一个外部的胃——我们叫它厨房。现在我们正在创造一个外部的大脑。这意味着什么，意义深远，而他也坦诚地承认：到目前为止，没有人真正知道答案。
