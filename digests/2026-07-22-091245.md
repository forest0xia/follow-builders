**Claude Blog: "Building intelligent apps for Apple platforms with Claude in the Foundation Models framework"**

Anthropic released a Swift package enabling Apple developers to use Apple's Foundation Models framework to call Claude for complex workflows. The integration lets developers tap into on-device Apple models for fast, local tasks like summarization or extraction, then seamlessly hand off to Claude when a request requires multi-step reasoning, code generation, web search, or code execution. **Training Data: "Factory's Matan Grinberg: The Coming 'Dark Factory' Where Software Builds Itself"**
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZesOukBjPmI

**The Takeaway:** Being two years early is the same as being wrong — but surviving those two years in the desert is what gives you the resilience to win when the market finally catches up.

Matan Grinberg is the co-founder and CEO of Factory, which builds "droids" — autonomous agents for software development. Factory started in April 2023, when the enterprise world was barely ready for GitHub Copilot, let alone fully autonomous agents. Their first two years were what Grinberg calls "the journey in the desert": they built for a use case enterprises weren't ready for, climbed to nearly $2M in revenue, realized developers weren't actually happy with the product, and proactively gave all their customers their money back. That decision — to reset rather than ride out mediocrity — forged the company's culture and kept the entire team intact through what Grinberg describes as one of the worst months of his life.

What changed wasn't primarily the models — it was developer behavior. When they launched the Droid CLI on September 26, 2025, two years after founding, the world had caught up: engineers were using autocomplete tools, becoming open to agentic workflows, and opinion leaders like Andrej Karpathy had publicly shifted toward embracing agents. "Being two or three years early is the same as being wrong," Grinberg said — the point being that consolation prizes don't exist in this business; only outcomes matter.

On competitive edge, Grinberg makes a counterintuitive argument: model-agnostic harnesses outperform model-specific ones. A harness exposed to multiple models avoids overfitting to any single model's quirks — analogous to how a model trained on the whole internet outperforms one trained only on your personal data. Factory's droids consistently outperform on benchmarks like TerminalBench compared to Claude Code or Codex running the same model natively.

His prediction for what's next: within 12-24 months, 90% of token consumption will be asynchronous — agents autonomously acting on signals without being explicitly invoked by a human. Today, nearly all consumption is still synchronous. The "dark factory" Grinberg envisions — software building itself in the background with the lights off, like a Tesla factory running at night — is where the company's name and vision have always pointed.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZesOukBjPmI

Matan Grinberg 是 Factory 的联创兼 CEO，Factory 构建"droids"——面向软件开发的自主 agent。Factory 于2023年4月创立，彼时企业界对 GitHub Copilot 都还未准备好，更遑论完全自主的 agent。最初两年，Grinberg 称之为"荒漠中的旅程"：他们为企业还没准备好的应用场景而构建，营收接近200万美元，却意识到开发者对产品并不真正满意，最终主动将所有款项退还给客户。这个选择——选择重置而非将就——锻造了公司文化，让整个团队在 Grinberg 描述的"人生最糟糕月份"中依然保持完整。

改变的根源并非主要来自模型本身，而是开发者行为的转变。2025年9月26日，创立两周年之际，他们发布了 Droid CLI——此时世界终于跟上来了：工程师们开始使用代码补全工具，对 agent 工作流逐渐开放，Andrej Karpathy 等意见领袖也公开转向拥抱 agent。"提前两三年等同于错了"，Grinberg 说——这个行业没有安慰奖，只有做成或没做成。

在竞争优势上，Grinberg 提出了一个反直觉论断：模型无关的 harness 性能优于模型专属的 harness。接触多个模型的 harness 不会过拟合于任何单一模型的特性——就像在整个互联网上训练的模型远比只在个人数据上训练的模型更强大。Factory droids 在 TerminalBench 等基准测试上的表现，始终优于 Claude Code 或 Codex 原生使用同一模型的结果。

他对未来的预测：在12-24个月内，90%的 token 消耗将是异步的——agent 自主响应信号采取行动，无需人类显式触发。如今几乎所有消耗仍是同步的。Grinberg 所描绘的"暗工厂"——如特斯拉夜间工厂一般在黑暗中自我运转的软件工厂——始终是公司名字与愿景所指向的方向。
