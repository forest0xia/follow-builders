**Aaron Levie (levie on X) — CEO of Box**

Box CEO Aaron Levie pushed back hard on the "agents will kill software jobs" narrative. He argues agents are the single biggest form of leverage for technical people in history — not because they'll replace developers, but because they'll create 100X more software than exists today. The unlocked demand includes legacy on-prem systems moving to cloud, SMBs that could never hire developers, security vulnerabilities that need patching at scale, and IT organizations automating workflows they never could before. His key nuance: agentic coding is a massive boon for technical professionals, but everyone rolling their own complex software with ongoing maintenance is a different matter.
