**Aaron Levie (Box CEO)**

Box CEO Aaron Levie identified a cognitive bias he's calling "AI Gell-Mann Amnesia": people who use AI for their own work can clearly see all the "last mile" friction — accessing data, adding context, reviewing outputs, integrating results into business processes — but when they look at someone else's job, they assume AI will fully automate it immediately. This, he argues, should make us skeptical of sweeping AI-driven job loss theories, since each role contains far more nuance than it appears from the outside.

In a separate thread, Levie also diagnosed two forces driving overwork in the AI era: (1) AI has dramatically raised the leverage on effort, making idle time feel like wasted team capacity — similar to a manager who can't stand having their team idle; and (2) AI has lowered the barrier to starting projects so much that people launch far more experiments, then spend disproportionate time finishing the last 10%. He predicts this will ultimately drive job creation as successful experiments get "promoted" to production processes.

Box CEO Aaron Levie 描述了一种他称之为"AI Gell-Mann 失忆症"的认知偏见：人们在用 AI 处理自己工作时，能清楚看到所有"最后一公里"的摩擦——获取数据、补充上下文、审核输出、将结果整合到业务流程中——但一转头看别人的工作，却以为 AI 会立刻全面替代它。他认为这应该让我们对 AI 大规模取代就业的论断保持怀疑，因为每个职位所包含的细节与复杂度，从外部看往往被严重低估。

**Garry Tan (YC President & CEO)**

YC President & CEO Garry Tan shared the architecture behind his highly articulate personal AI agent — and it's three files, not one. SOUL.md defines voice, values, and operating principles in brutal specificity ("brevity is mandatory," "humor is mandatory," "never open with 'Great question,'" "swearing is allowed when it lands"). USER.md is a deep model of the user — not a bio but a ~4,000-word account of how their mind works, what they're building, blind spots, temperament, and what they care about. AGENTS.md is the operational playbook: what to check every message, what to never do, how to handle failures. His verdict: "Generic instructions → generic output. If you write 'be helpful and concise' you get ChatGPT. If you write 'speak like a peer with taste, one sentence when one sentence works, uncomfortable truths welcome if actually true, language with voltage' — you get something alive."

He also noted with amusement that his OpenClaw agent, knowing his full schedule and health goals, refuses to respond to queries after 12:30am.

**Anthropic Engineering — Claude Code auto mode: a safer way to skip permissions**

Anthropic Engineering introduces Claude Code's new "auto mode" — a middle ground between manual permission prompts (which create approval fatigue; users accept 93% of them anyway) and the dangerous `--dangerously-skip-permissions` flag. Auto mode delegates approval decisions to two AI-powered layers: a server-side prompt-injection probe that scans tool outputs before they enter the agent's context, and a two-stage transcript classifier (running on Sonnet 4.6) that evaluates each action before execution.

The classifier runs a fast single-token filter first (stage 1: 8.5% false positive rate, catches most dangerous actions), then chain-of-thought reasoning only when stage 1 flags something (stage 2: drops false positives to 0.4%). The post is honest about the tradeoff: "The 17% false-negative rate on real overeager actions is the honest number." Key design choice: the classifier sees only user messages and bare tool calls — no assistant text, no tool results — making it resistant to manipulation and prompt injection. When an action is blocked, Claude gets a denial and is instructed to find a safer path rather than halting; after 3 consecutive or 20 total denials, it escalates to a human.
https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/claude-code-auto-mode

Anthropic Engineering 推出了 Claude Code 的全新"auto mode"（自动模式）——这是手动审批提示（会造成审批疲劳，用户反正也会接受其中 93%）与危险的 `--dangerously-skip-permissions` 标志之间的折中方案。Auto mode 将审批决策委托给两个 AI 驱动的层次：一个在工具输出进入 agent 上下文之前扫描它们的服务端提示注入探测器，以及一个在每个操作执行前进行评估的两阶段转录分类器（运行在 Sonnet 4.6 上）。

分类器首先运行快速的单 token 过滤器（阶段 1：误报率 8.5%，能捕获大多数危险操作），然后只在第一阶段标记时才进行思维链推理（阶段 2：将误报率降低到 0.4%）。这篇文章对权衡取舍有着难得的坦诚："真实过度积极操作的 17% 漏报率，这是诚实的数字。"关键设计选择：分类器只看到用户消息和裸 tool call——没有 assistant 文本，没有工具结果——使其能抵御操控和提示注入。当一个操作被拦截时，Claude 会收到拒绝结果并被指示寻找更安全的路径，而不是停止运行；在连续 3 次或累计 20 次拒绝后，会话升级给人类处理。

**AI & I by Every — "The AI Model Built for What LLMs Can't Do"**

**The Takeaway:** Energy-based models (EBMs) aren't a replacement for LLMs — they're what LLMs fundamentally can't be: non-autoregressive, token-free, verifiable from the inside, and constrained by design.

Eve, founder and CEO of Logical Intelligence, makes a compelling case that the AI industry has been using the wrong tool for a broad class of problems. Her company's model, Kona (formally an "energy-based reasoning model with latent variables"), takes a radically different approach from transformers: instead of predicting the next token, it constructs an "energy landscape" — a map of states from low-probability to high-probability outcomes — and navigates it directly. No tokens. No guessing game.

The core insight is almost physical: "EBMs are naturally non-autoregressive. There are no sequences of tokens, and that's what makes it fundamentally different. Like, imagine you're trying to navigate the map, and you have a left brain... EBM gonna have the bird view all the time. So if you see there's a hole, you're gonna choose a different route."

This architectural difference translates to real advantages: EBMs can be formally constrained, verified internally during training, and work well with sparse data. Eve identifies a massive untapped gap — industries like drug discovery, energy grid management, and financial data analysis are almost entirely unautomated today. The reason isn't that the problems aren't solvable; it's that enterprises won't share sensitive data with large B2C LLMs and can't tolerate hallucination risk. EBMs are built for precisely this space.

Crucially, Logical Intelligence isn't trying to replace LLMs — it's positioning itself as a complementary layer. When an LLM hits a spatial reasoning or data analysis problem, it can outsource to an EBM. And the signal that large LLM companies are building EBMs in-house? "That's a positive signal for us."

**AI & I by Every — "The AI Model Built for What LLMs Can't Do"**

**核心观点：** Energy-based model（EBM）不是 LLM 的替代品——它们是 LLM 从根本上无法成为的东西：非自回归、无 token、可从内部验证，且在设计上可被约束。

Logical Intelligence 创始人兼 CEO Eve 提出了一个有力论点：AI 行业一直在用错误的工具解决一大类问题。她公司的模型 Kona（正式名称为"带潜变量的基于能量的推理模型"）与 transformer 采用了截然不同的方法：它不预测下一个 token，而是构建一个"能量景观"——一张从低概率到高概率结果的状态地图——并直接在其上导航。没有 token，没有猜测游戏。

这个核心洞察几乎来自物理学："EBM 天然是非自回归的。没有 token 序列，这就是它根本不同的原因。就像你在地图上导航，你用的是左脑……EBM 始终拥有鸟瞰视角。所以如果你看到前面有个坑，你会选择另一条路。"

这种架构差异转化为实际优势：EBM 可以被正式约束、在训练过程中从内部验证，并且能很好地处理稀疏数据。Eve 指出了一个巨大的未开发空白——药物发现、能源电网管理、金融数据分析等行业今天几乎完全没有被自动化。原因不是问题无法解决，而是企业不愿将敏感数据分享给大型 B2C LLM，也无法容忍幻觉风险。EBM 正是为这个空间而生的。

关键一点是，Logical Intelligence 并不打算取代 LLM——而是将自己定位为互补的一层。当 LLM 遇到空间推理或数据分析问题时，可以将其外包给 EBM。至于大型 LLM 公司正在内部构建 EBM 这一信号？"这对我们来说是个积极信号。"
