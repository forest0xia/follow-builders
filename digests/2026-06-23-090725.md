### AI & I by Every — "How Anthropic Uses Claude Fable 5 With Mike Krieger"

**The Takeaway:** The gap between idea and working software has collapsed so dramatically that even a co-founder of Instagram now delegates complex engineering tasks overnight to Claude — and wakes up to find them done.

Mike Krieger is the head of Anthropic Labs and co-founder of Instagram, which means he has an unusually calibrated sense of what "fast" and "productive" look like in software. His conversation with Every's Dan Shipper is the clearest window yet into how Anthropic's own builders actually use Fable 5 day-to-day — not the demo version, but the month-two version.

The shift Krieger identifies isn't about what Fable can do — it's about how fundamentally your interaction model has to change to use it well. Early on, he found himself prompting the way he always had, decomposing tasks into small interactive chunks. Then he realized the model had outgrown that approach. Now he starts with long architectural planning conversations, has Claude produce an HTML diagram or markdown doc to align the team, then delegates extended chunks of work with multiple concurrent sessions. "I'll wish Claude a good night, set it up on a pretty complex task, and wake up to it usually done by two in the morning."

The demo he shows is a personal media tracker he built over a weekend — built on top of Anthropic's Managed Agents so it can modify its own code from within a chat interface. The most impressive part: a long-press UI that submits edit requests, previews diffs, and live-reloads on his phone via Expo tooling. This wasn't a polished product pitch — it was a working side project built in stolen hours between hiking with his kids.

Krieger's sharpest observation is about democratization. A recruiter at Anthropic came to him after being given internal tooling that combined Fable with access to internal MCPs. She told him: "It is the first time in my life where the thing that's in my head and the thing that exists in the world is now right next to each other. Like, I can just do it." Previously, that person would have spent months on an internal tools backlog. Now she's building.

On whether software engineering is "over": his answer is careful. The role is fundamentally changed — less time in the editor, more architectural judgment, more managing parallel agent sessions. But the taste, the sense of what good infra looks like at each stage of scale, and the ability to verify results — those haven't gone anywhere. "Software engineering is different. It is dramatically changed. But the human capacity for creativity is enormous."

For the model itself, Krieger notes that Fable is the first model where he's actually experimented with effort levels — dropping to medium for minor UI tweaks, reserving full reasoning for genuinely hard architectural decisions. And he's learned to route trivial questions elsewhere (he switched his iOS app to Sonnet for everyday queries). The meta-skill, he suggests, isn't prompting — it's knowing which model deserves your question.
https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLuMcoKK9mKgHtW_o9h5sGO2vXrffKHwJL

**核心观点：** 从想法到可运行软件之间的差距已经缩小到如此惊人的程度——就连 Instagram 联合创始人现在也会在夜里把复杂的工程任务委托给 Claude，第二天早上醒来发现一切都完成了。

Mike Krieger 是 Anthropic Labs 负责人、Instagram 联合创始人。这意味着他对"快速"和"高效"在软件领域意味着什么，有着异常精准的参照系。他与 Every 的 Dan Shipper 的对话，是迄今为止最清晰的窗口，让我们得以了解 Anthropic 自己的建造者在日常工作中究竟如何使用 Fable 5——不是演示版本，而是用了两个月之后的版本。

Krieger 指出的转变，不在于 Fable 能做什么，而在于要用好它，你的交互模式必须发生多么根本性的改变。一开始，他仍按照以往的方式提示模型，把任务分解为细小的交互片段。后来他意识到，模型已经超越了这种方式。现在，他先进行长时间的架构规划对话，让 Claude 生成 HTML 图表或 Markdown 文档来统一团队认知，然后通过多个并行 session 委托扩展的工作块。"我会跟 Claude 道晚安，给它布置一个相当复杂的任务，然后醒来发现通常凌晨两点就已经完成了。"

他展示的 demo 是一个在周末构建的个人媒体追踪器——基于 Anthropic 的 Managed Agents，可以通过聊天界面修改自身代码。最令人印象深刻的是：一个长按 UI，用于提交编辑请求、预览 diff，并通过 Expo 工具链在他的手机上实现热重载。这不是精心打磨的产品展示，而是在带孩子爬山的间隙抽空完成的一个真实运行的副业项目。

Krieger 最犀利的观察是关于民主化。Anthropic 内部一名负责招聘的同事，在获得了结合 Fable 和内部 MCP 访问权限的内部工具后，告诉他："这是我人生中第一次，我脑子里想的和现实中存在的东西，几乎紧挨在一起了。就是，我能直接做到了。" 以前，这位同事需要等几个月才能排到内部工具工程师的档期。现在，她正享受着构建的乐趣。

关于软件工程是否已经"终结"，他的回答很审慎。这个角色已经发生了根本性的变化——在编辑器里花的时间越来越少，架构判断越来越重要，管理并行 agent session 越来越日常。但对于"什么才是好的基础设施"的品味、在规模化不同阶段的判断力，以及验证结果的能力——这些并没有消失。"软件工程不同了，它发生了翻天覆地的变化。但人类的创造力容量是无穷的。"

在模型本身的使用上，Krieger 提到 Fable 是他真正开始实验 effort 等级设置的第一个模型——对于细小的 UI 调整，他会调到 medium；真正困难的架构决策才动用完整的推理能力。对于琐碎的问题，他学会了换模型（已把 iOS app 的日常问答切换到 Sonnet）。他的元认知技能，不再是怎么写 prompt，而是：哪个问题值得让哪个模型来回答。
