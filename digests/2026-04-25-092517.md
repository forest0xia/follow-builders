**Anthropic Engineering: Scaling Managed Agents: Decoupling the brain from the hands**

Anthropic Engineering published a deep architectural post on Managed Agents, their hosted service for long-horizon agents. The core problem: enterprise harnesses encode assumptions about what Claude can't do, and those assumptions go stale as models improve. Their solution was to decouple the "brain" (Claude and its harness) from the "hands" (sandboxes and tools) and the "session" (the append-only event log) — modeled on how operating systems virtualized hardware into stable abstractions that outlasted generations of hardware changes.

The structural benefits are concrete. Decoupling reduced p50 time-to-first-token by roughly 60% and p95 by over 90%, since containers are now provisioned on-demand only when needed rather than upfront. Security improved structurally as well: the sandbox where Claude's generated code runs is now physically separated from credentials, eliminating a class of prompt injection attacks. The session log lives outside Claude's context window as a durable "context object," letting the harness selectively fetch and transform context without irreversible compaction decisions.

"Operating systems have lasted decades by virtualizing the hardware into abstractions general enough for programs that didn't exist yet. Managed Agents follows the same pattern."
https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/managed-agents

Anthropic Engineering 发表了一篇关于 Managed Agents 架构的深度技术文章。核心问题在于：企业 harness 会将"Claude 做不到什么"的假设编码进去，而随着模型不断进化，这些假设会逐渐过时。他们的解决方案是将"大脑"（Claude 及其 harness）与"双手"（沙箱和工具）以及"会话"（追加式事件日志）完全解耦——设计灵感来自操作系统将硬件虚拟化为稳定抽象层，使抽象层比底层硬件更为长寿。

架构解耦带来了显著的量化收益：p50 首 token 延迟降低约 60%，p95 降低超过 90%，因为容器现在按需分配而非预先部署。安全性也在结构层面得到改善：Claude 生成代码运行的沙箱与凭证实现了物理隔离，从根本上消除了一类 prompt 注入攻击向量。会话日志作为持久化"上下文对象"存储于 Claude 上下文窗口之外，让 harness 可以按需获取和转换上下文，同时避免不可逆的压缩决策。

"操作系统之所以历经数十年而不衰，是因为它将硬件虚拟化为足够通用的抽象，以服务于尚未诞生的程序。Managed Agents 遵循同样的模式。"
https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/managed-agents

**No Priors — SAP: Bringing the 'Operating System' of a Company into the AI Era with CTO Philipp Herzig**

**The Takeaway:** LLMs are not enough for enterprise AI — predictive, tabular data problems require specialized models, and the real challenge isn't building the AI, it's teaching it to do the right thing at scale across 20,000 APIs and millions of customers.

SAP CTO Philipp Herzig brings a decidedly un-hyped view of enterprise AI adoption. SAP runs the financial, HR, and supply chain systems for 400,000 companies worldwide — what Herzig calls "the operating system of a company." He keeps returning to a widening gap he observes between the AI innovation race and the AI outcome race: the former is accelerating fast; the latter is not keeping up.

His most contrarian point: LLMs excel at unstructured text and images, but enterprise decisions run on predictions — demand forecasting, cash flow modeling, payment delay classification. These are tabular, structured problems that token-by-token language models simply weren't designed to solve. SAP is investing heavily in specialized predictive models alongside LLMs, because XGBoost and AutoML approaches have scale challenges of their own that sequence-to-sequence generation can't address.

The evals parallel is sharp: agentic coding works because you can verify if code compiles and tests pass. Enterprise AI needs the same rigor. SAP is building evaluation datasets from decades of transaction records — constructing ground truth for "given this input, what's the verified correct output?" He calls the ongoing process "agent mining": capturing every human clarification and decision trace to improve agent behavior over time in a data flywheel.

"The problem is not the AI so much. It's actually teaching the AI to do the right thing at scale."

**No Priors — SAP: Bringing the 'Operating System' of a Company into the AI Era with CTO Philipp Herzig**

**核心洞察：** LLM 不足以支撑企业级 AI——表格数据的预测任务需要专业化模型，而真正的挑战不在于构建 AI 本身，而在于如何在 2 万个 API 和数百万客户中实现规模化、正确的落地。

SAP CTO Philipp Herzig 对企业 AI 采用持一种务实克制的态度。SAP 为全球 40 万家企业运营财务、人力资源和供应链系统——Herzig 将其称为"公司的操作系统"。他反复提到一个他观察到的不断扩大的鸿沟：AI 创新竞赛与 AI 成果竞赛之间的差距——前者在加速，后者却未能跟上。

他最有反主流意味的观点是：LLM 在处理非结构化文本和图像方面表现卓越，但企业决策依赖预测——需求预测、现金流建模、付款延迟分类。这些都是表格结构化问题，token 逐一生成的语言模型并非为此而设计。SAP 正在同步大力投资专业化预测模型与 LLM，因为 XGBoost 和 AutoML 方法有其自身的规模化挑战，而序列到序列生成无法解决这些问题。

他对 eval 的类比十分精到：agent 编码之所以有效，是因为你可以验证代码是否编译、测试是否通过。企业 AI 需要同样的严谨性。SAP 正在从数十年的交易记录中构建评估数据集，为"给定输入，什么是经过验证的正确输出"建立基准。他将持续进行中的这一过程称为"agent mining"：捕捉每一次人工澄清和决策轨迹，在数据飞轮中持续改进 agent 行为。

"问题不在于 AI 本身，而在于如何教会 AI 在规模层面做正确的事。"
